Purpose of Logo

The purpose of the logo is predominantely for brand recognition. The design is simple with a key word and a flamingo icon. The choice of design and unique icon are strategic to encourage memorability and establish association with the brand.

Naturally humans associate features such as colours, images and fonts with certain emotions and products which impacts interpretation of logos. For this reason the colours and fonts were chosen with the users first impressions and expectations of the brand after exposure in mind. One demonstration is the choice of colours, they are not visually overwhelming but offer enough of a contrast for the viewer to be able to focus on the key messages the logo wants to communicate. In colour theory brown and beige represent trust, dependability and quality which is inkeeping with the brands values of 'Wellbeing, Reliability, Honesty & Education'.

Another example is the choice of font (Playfair Display). The font is classic and identifies well with the brand, it doesn't indicate corporate, radical aspects. The use of the keyword #goals communicates a message to the viewer that this brand will aid the consumer in achieving some kind of goal.
